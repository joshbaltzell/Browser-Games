## Verification

Open `games/neon-swarm/index.html` directly from `file://` (double-click) and start a game. Obtain at least one Last Stand charge (requires the `laststand` fusion skill in the skill tree) and then take lethal damage. Observe the sequence:

1. **Time freeze (V01):** The instant Last Stand fires, all motion — enemies, bullets, particles — halts completely for a clearly perceptible pause (~0.4s wall-clock time). The player does not die.

2. **White radial bloom (V02):** A white radial glow erupts from the player at the freeze moment and fades out over the freeze window. At freeze start the bloom is near-opaque; by freeze end it has faded to invisible.

3. **Six lightning cracks (V03):** Six white lightning-arc lines radiate outward from the player in a star/asterisk pattern (every 60°, equal angles), each approximately 180px long. They fade over ~0.6s. Color is white, visually distinct from the cyan of chain-lightning.

4. **Snap-back burst (V04):** After the freeze ends, the game world visibly accelerates — enemies and bullets lurch forward at a noticeably faster rate for ~0.3s before settling.

5. **Smooth lerp to normal speed (V05):** After the snap burst, speed decelerates smoothly over ~0.5s back to normal (no abrupt jump to 1×).

6. **Audio sweep (V06):** A rising tone plays at the freeze moment — a clearly audible triangle-wave sweep from low to high pitch lasting ~0.4s, at moderate volume. No bomb sound plays. Toggle mute to confirm the sweep is silenced when muted.

7. **No timeScale leak (V07):** After the full sequence (total ~1.2s), `timeScale` is exactly 1.0 and all game systems run at normal speed. Verify by watching enemy movement rate return fully to baseline.

8. **No regression on normal gameplay (V08):** Without triggering Last Stand, all existing mechanics — movement, firing, slowmo powerup, overdrive powerup, freeze powerup — behave exactly as before. No console errors.

9. **Multiple triggers (V09):** If a player has multiple Last Stand charges, triggering the cinematic a second time (on a subsequent lethal hit) restarts the full sequence cleanly without leftover timer state from the first trigger.

10. **File:// protocol (V10):** No ES module imports, no fetch calls, no build step required. The game opens by double-clicking `index.html` and all cinematic behavior is present.
