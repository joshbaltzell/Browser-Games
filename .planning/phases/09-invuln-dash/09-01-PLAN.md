## Verification

Open `games/neon-swarm/index.html` directly from `file://` (double-click) and click Start. Then:

1. **Dash moves the player (SC1):** Hold a movement direction (e.g. hold Right / D) and tap Shift. The player teleports ~120px in that direction in a single instant. Try each of the 8 directions (cardinals + diagonals) — direction matches held keys.
2. **Invuln frames (SC2):** Dash directly into a cluster of enemies. During the dash window (~0.35s) the player flickers and takes no contact damage (HP bar does not drop while flickering).
3. **Cooldown + HUD indicator (SC3):** Immediately after a dash, press Shift again — nothing happens. The `⚡ DASH` HUD indicator dims to ~30% opacity right after dashing and brightens back to full after ~1.5s; a second dash is only possible once it is bright again.
4. **Afterimage trail (SC4):** On each dash, 3 cyan glowing ghost circles appear along the dash path and fade out within ~0.25s.
5. **No-direction guard (SC5):** Standing still (no movement key held), tap Shift repeatedly. The player does NOT move and the cooldown does NOT start (indicator stays bright).
6. **No regressions:** Normal movement, auto-aim firing, enemy behavior, level-up, and power-ups all behave exactly as before. No console errors. Confirm taking a normal hit still grants the existing 0.6s invuln (the dash's 0.35s set does not break the damage path).
