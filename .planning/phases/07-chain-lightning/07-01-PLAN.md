# Phase 07: Chain Lightning Upgrade — Plan 01

## Goal

Add Chain Lightning as a new entry in the `UPGRADES` array. On bullet impact, if the
player has `chainCount > 0`, the bullet's damage arcs to the nearest OTHER enemy within
150px for 55% of the primary hit's damage, falling off 0.55× per additional hop. Each
stack of the upgrade adds one more hop. A jagged cyan arc visual briefly connects each
pair of chained enemies and fades over ~0.15s. No existing upgrades or mechanics change.

All work happens in `games/neon-swarm/game.js`. No DOM changes — the upgrade card UI is
data-driven from the `UPGRADES` array, so `index.html` is untouched.

   be killed by the chain — and a small cyan particle burst appears at the chained target,

   the arc now hops twice (hit → enemy A → enemy B), each hop dealing less than the previous.

   appears (chain stops when nothing is in range).
6. Land a crit on a primary target with Chain Lightning active: confirm the chained hit is
   NOT crit-boosted (chain damage stays at 55% of base, gold crit visuals only on the
   primary).
7. Spray into a large dense swarm: confirm no console errors, no visible frame drops, and
   arcs never appear to stack up indefinitely (cap of 12 active arcs).
8. Confirm existing upgrades (splash, pierce, crit, orbital, etc.) and power-ups still behave
   exactly as before — no regressions.
